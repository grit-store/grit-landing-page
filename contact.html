Shipping & Returns

📦

Shipping Policy

  • All items are printed and made-to-order especially for you.
  • Fulfillment takes 2–4 business days before shipping.
  • Delivery takes 3–7 business days across India.
  • Tracking link will be sent via email or SMS once shipped.
  • Customer is responsible for reshipping fees of undeliverable RTO packages.
  • We currently ship within India only.
🔄

Returns & Refunds

  • No returns or exchanges for buyer's remorse or sizing issues.
  • Damaged or defective items are eligible for free replacement.
  • Report issues to support@gritnation.online within 7 days of delivery.
  • Include order number, clear photos of the defect, and an unboxing video.
  • Approved replacements are shipped to you at no extra cost.
  • Order cancellations are only accepted within 24 hours.
