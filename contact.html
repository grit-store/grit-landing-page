Shipping & Returns

📦

Shipping Policy

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days.
  • Delivery takes 3–7 business days across India.
  • Free shipping on orders above ₹5,000.
  • Standard shipping: ₹99 for orders below ₹5,000.
  • You will receive a tracking number via email once shipped.
  • We currently ship within India only.
🔄

Returns & Refunds

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, with tags attached.
  • Sale items are not eligible for returns.
  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • To initiate: email us at support@grit.in with order ID.
