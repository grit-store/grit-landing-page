Shipping Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Thank you for choosing GRIT. We are dedicated to delivering our premium streetwear products to you quickly and securely. Please read below for all details regarding our shipping processes, timelines, and rates.

1. Processing & Fulfillment Times

Since every GRIT product is custom-printed to order to minimize material waste and maintain extreme quality, our printing and processing times are as follows:

  • Processing Time: 2 to 3 business days (printing, packing, and quality checks).
  • Orders placed on weekends or national holidays will begin processing on the next working business day.

2. Shipping Rates & Destinations

We deliver to over 20,000+ pin codes across India through premier courier partners (Delhivery, BlueDart, Xpressbees, etc.).

  • Standard Shipping: Flat ₹80 shipping charge for orders under ₹5,000.
  • Free Shipping: Free shipping is automatically unlocked on all orders of ₹5,000 and above.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available at an additional flat convenience fee of ₹40 per order to cover carrier cash-handling charges.

3. Delivery Timelines

Once your order has finished processing and is handed over to our shipping carriers, the typical transit times are:

  • Metro & Tier 1 Cities: 2 to 4 business days.
  • Rest of India: 3 to 7 business days.
  • Northeast India & Remote Regions: 5 to 10 business days.

*Please note: total time to reach your doorstep = Processing time (2-3 days) + Transit time (2-5 days). We estimate a total of 5 to 8 business days for standard delivery across India.*

4. Order Tracking

As soon as your package ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email and/or WhatsApp update containing your unique Tracking ID. You can track your package directly on our white-labeled tracking page or through the carrier's official website.

5. Undelivered / Returned to Origin (RTO) Shipments

If a package is returned to our fulfillment facility because of an incorrect/incomplete shipping address, or if the customer is not contactable after multiple delivery attempts by our courier partners, the shipment is classified as RTO (Returned to Origin).

We can reship RTO orders back to you, but a standard reshipping charge of ₹80 will be applicable to cover courier charges. Please contact our support team immediately at support@gritnation.online if you see delivery issues on your tracking link.

6. Contact Us

For any questions or support regarding your order shipment, feel free to reach out to our dedicated shipping desk at support@gritnation.online.
