Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for choosing GRIT. We are dedicated to delivering our premium streetwear products to you quickly and securely. Please read below for all details regarding our shipping processes, timelines, and tracking.

1. Order Processing Time

Since all our items are made-to-order especially for you, please allow 2 to 4 business days for your order to be printed, quality-checked, and packed before it is shipped. Processing times may be slightly longer during holidays or major sales events.

2. Shipping Delivery Times

We partner with reliable courier services across India. Once your order is dispatched, the estimated delivery time is 3 to 7 business days, depending on your location:

  • Metro cities typically see faster delivery.
  • Remote or non-metro areas may take a few extra days.

3. Order Tracking

As soon as your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email or SMS containing your tracking number. You can use this to keep an eye on your package's journey.

4. Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address provided by the customer or repeated failed delivery attempts by the courier, we are happy to reship the item. However, the customer will be responsible for the reshipping costs.

5. Contact Us

For any questions or support regarding your order shipment, feel free to reach out to our dedicated support desk at support@gritnation.online.
