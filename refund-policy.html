Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At GRIT, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, custom-printed apparel and accessories. Because every item is printed uniquely for you after you place your order, we do not hold inventory and cannot accept returns or exchanges for buyer's remorse, incorrect color choices, or sizing issues.

Please refer closely to our size charts before placing your order to ensure the perfect fit!

📹 Recommended: Record an Unboxing Video

An unboxing video is highly recommended as it helps us verify transit damage or printing errors and process replacement claims with our printing partners much faster.

1. Damaged or Defective Items

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you receive an item that is defective, damaged during transit, or has a manufacturing or printing error, we will gladly offer a free replacement.

To be eligible for a replacement, please follow these steps:

  1. Report it quickly: Contact us at support@gritnation.online within 7 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide proof: Include clear photos of the defect, the damaged packaging (if applicable), and an unboxing video.
  3. Include your details: Don't forget to include your Order Number in the email.

Once we review and approve the issue, a replacement will be processed and shipped to you at no extra cost.

2. Order Cancellations

Because orders are sent to production very quickly, cancellations are only accepted within 24 hours of placing the order. Once an order has entered the printing stage, it cannot be canceled or modified.

3. Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address provided by the customer or repeated failed delivery attempts by the courier, we are happy to reship the item. However, the customer will be responsible for the reshipping costs.
