Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At GRIT, we take immense pride in the premium quality of our streetwear and apparel. Since each piece is custom-printed to order just for you, we want to ensure you have a transparent and smooth experience should any issue arise.

📹 Important: Unboxing Video Requirement

To protect against fraudulent claims and ensure smooth processing, we kindly request our customers to record a continuous unboxing video of their package from the moment it is sealed to the reveal of the item. This is mandatory for processing any damage, wrong item, or size mismatch claims.

1. Returns & Replacements Eligibility

We offer a **7-Day Return and Replacement Policy** from the date of delivery. We only accept returns or replacements under the following circumstances:

  • The product received has a manufacturing defect (e.g., faulty print, stitching issues).
  • The product received was damaged during transit.
  • The product received is completely different from what was ordered (wrong size, wrong design, or wrong color).

To be eligible for a return or replacement, your item must be unused, unwashed, and in the same condition that you received it, with all original tags and packaging intact.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Because our products are made-to-order, we cannot accept returns, refunds, or exchanges in the following cases:

  • Change of mind or preference (e.g., "I don't want this design anymore").
  • Wrong size ordered by the customer (please double-check our detailed Size Chart on each product page before ordering!).
  • Items that have been washed, worn, or modified in any way.

3. How to Initiate a Return or Replacement

If your order qualifies for a return or replacement based on the criteria above, please follow these steps:

  1. Email our support team at support@gritnation.online within 7 days of receiving the package.
  2. Include your **Order Number**, details of the issue, and attach your continuous **Unboxing Video** or high-resolution photos showing the defect clearly.
  3. Our Quality Control team will review your request within 24–48 hours. Once approved, we will arrange a reverse pickup or provide further instructions.

4. Refunds

Once your returned item is received and inspected by our warehouse partners (Qikink), we will send you an email to notify you that we have received it. If approved, your refund will be processed and automatically credited back to your original payment method (Bank Account, UPI, or Card) within **5 to 7 working days**.

5. Cancellations

Since printing begins within hours of placing your order, cancellations are only accepted if requested within **2 hours** of placing the order. Please email support@gritnation.online immediately if you need to cancel your order.
