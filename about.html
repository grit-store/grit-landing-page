The Story

GRIT was founded on a simple frustration — most clothing was either cheap and disposable, or expensive and pretentious. There was no middle ground for people who wanted to dress well without overthinking it.

We set out to change that. Every piece in the GRIT lineup is designed with intention: clean silhouettes, premium materials, and cuts that work in the real world — whether you're at a meeting, at the gym, or just living your day.

We believe your wardrobe should reflect who you are, not what's trending. That's why we don't follow fast fashion. We create essentials that are built to last, season after season.
